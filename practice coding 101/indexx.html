Interesting Facts About Earth

Earth's Size

Earth has a diameter of about 12,742 km, making it the fifth-largest planet in our solar system.

Water Abundance

About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water, mostly oceans.

Atmosphere Layers

Earth's atmosphere is composed of multiple layers that protect life and regulate temperature.

Unique Features

Earth is the only known planet to support life, thanks to its unique combination of atmosphere, liquid water, and suitable temperature.

  • Dynamic climate system
  • Plate tectonics shaping the surface
  • Diverse ecosystems from deserts to rainforests
  • Magnetic field protecting from solar radiation
